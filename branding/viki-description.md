# VIKI — Voice Intelligence Neural Interface

VIKI is a local, voice-first AI brainstorming partner built on Claude. She listens continuously, thinks out loud with you, and captures every exchange so no idea gets lost between the moment it's spoken and the moment it gets built.

Designed for thinkers who move faster than they type, VIKI bridges the gap between raw creative thinking and real implementation. Brainstorm with her by voice, then push sessions directly into Claude Code as structured markdown references turning conversations into buildable context.

VIKI runs entirely on your machine, speaks with a natural AI voice via ElevenLabs, and syncs every session to Supabase so your ideas are always accessible wherever you build.

**Think it. Say it. Build it.**
